## Controls

Controls are as follows:

Key | Usage
--- | -----
W | Forward
S | Backward
A | Left
D | Right
Left click | Shoot
Right Click | Scope in
Left Shift | Run
Q | Previous weapon
E | Next weapon
U | Use MedPack (+10 HP)
H | Use Potion (Restore HP)
Esc | Pause
